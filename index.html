India's confectionery market — largely untapped by global formats

There are flavours and formats that are mainstream across Asia, Europe, and the Americas — that have never had a serious home in India. We're changing that.

India's confectionery market is one of the largest in the world, and still largely defined by the same few formats it's had for decades. The consumer has moved. The products haven't.

We are a funded, full-time venture operating out of Delhi NCR. We are building our core team now, ahead of launch — looking for people ready to own functions, not just fill them.
